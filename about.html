Future Plans Mastodon

Mastodon will be a hefty investment on my part.

AWS will cost around $65 a month to run a 1-50 user instance, which amounts to about $780-$1000 a year.

I will still be keeping the orbistwitter.com domain for two years. But this Mastodon instance will be costly and probably impossible to maintain alone.

The Mastodon instance will only be possible if I get donations and people willing to help set up; that's right, I need a small team of maybe 5 people who are willing to donate their precious time.

The Mastodon instance has the possibility of bringing more traffic to P&W itself and serving as the official "forum" for the game. It will also change the game greatly.

As for now, this is an investment too big for my wallet. If it cost around $12/month for 500 users, I would continue, but that's not the case.

Thanks for your time and understanding!!! But hey, at least we can call ourselves orbistwitter.com because that's ours!!!
