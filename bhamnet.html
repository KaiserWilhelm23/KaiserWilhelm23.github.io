Buckingham County 2m Net

@ 147.550 MHz FM

About

This net was created to serve the Buckingham County area and portions of the surrounding areas.

Info

The net meets on Saturdays and Sundays at 8 PM on 147.550 MHz FM.

The net frequency can also serve as a county wide simplex throughout the day.

On week days @ 8PM there will be a round table of info sharing with no Net Control..

Please read our Basic Net Protocols & Rules

This net is not endorsed by county officials it is run by the citizens!!!!
