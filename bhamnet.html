Buckingham County 2m Net

    @ 155.550 MHz FM

    About

    This net was created to serve the Buckingham County area and portions of the surrounding areas.

    Info

    The net meets on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays at 8 PM on 155.550 MHz FM. The net frequency can also serve as a County wide simplex throughout the day. On off days @ 8PM there will be a round table of info sharing with no Net Control. This excludes Sundays.
